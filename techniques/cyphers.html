Caesar Cyphers

Caesar ciphers are one of the simplest substitution cipher encryption techniques, each letter in a set of text is shifted by a set amount. For example the letter "A" with a positive shift of 1 becomes "B", likewise "A" with a negative shift, loops around and becomes a "Z"
